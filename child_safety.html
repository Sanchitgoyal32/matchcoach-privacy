MatchCoach — Child Safety Standards

Last updated: May 1, 2026

Commitment to Child Safety

MatchCoach is committed to the safety and protection of children. We have zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) of any kind on our platform.

Age Requirement

MatchCoach is intended exclusively for users aged 18 and above. The app is designed to assist adults with dating app conversations and is not intended for use by minors.

Prevention of Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE)

MatchCoach does not host, store, or distribute user-generated content, images, or media on any server. All images (screenshots and profile photos) are processed temporarily for AI analysis and are not stored permanently on our servers. This architecture inherently prevents the distribution of any exploitative material through our platform.

Detection and Reporting

If we become aware of any child sexual abuse material (CSAM) or any form of child exploitation connected to our app, we will:

1. Immediately remove and block the content or user.

2. Report the incident to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) via CyberTipline.

3. Report to relevant local and national authorities as required by law.

4. Cooperate fully with law enforcement investigations.

User Reporting

Users can report any child safety concerns by contacting us directly at: matchcoachapp@gmail.com

All reports are treated with the highest priority and will be reviewed and acted upon promptly.

Compliance

MatchCoach complies with all applicable child safety laws and regulations, including but not limited to the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and relevant Indian laws including the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences Act (POCSO).
